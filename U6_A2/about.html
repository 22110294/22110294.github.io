About our Company

About Us

Celtec is an innovative independent game development studio based in Wrexham, North East Wales. We specialize in creating captivating gaming experiences across desktop, mobile, and all major consoles including PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo Switch. At Celtec Games, we merge creativity with technical expertise, delivering titles that engage, inspire, and entertain players worldwide. Whether you’re a casual gamer or a hardcore enthusiast, we believe in building immersive worlds that everyone can enjoy.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to elevate the gaming experience by producing bold, imaginative, and inclusive games that resonate with global audiences. We push the boundaries of innovation using cutting-edge technology and storytelling to bring meaningful and unforgettable adventures to life!
